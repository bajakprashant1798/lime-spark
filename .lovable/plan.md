# My Fit Boat — Lime Electrolyte Drink Website

## Overview

A modern, single-product website for My Fit Boat's lime-flavored electrolyte drink, inspired by Supply6's clean, bold design style. Uses a teal/green + white color scheme to match the reference site and complement the lime flavor.

## Pages

### 1. Home Page (Landing)

- **Announcement bar** — scrolling ticker at the top (e.g., "Stay Hydrated. Stay Strong.")
- **Navigation** — My Fit Boat logo (uploaded), links to sections: Product, Benefits, Ingredients, FAQ, and a prominent "Order Now" CTA button
- **Hero section** — Full-width bold hero with large product headline ("Hydrate Like a Pro"), subtext about the lime electrolyte drink, and a CTA button. Placeholder area for product image
- **Why My Fit Boat section** — 3-4 feature cards highlighting key benefits (Rapid Hydration, Zero Sugar, Natural Lime Flavor, Essential Electrolytes)
- **Nutrition & Ingredients section** — Clean breakdown of electrolyte content, calories, ingredients list with icons/badges
- **Trust & Certifications section** — Trust badges (Lab Tested, Natural Ingredients, etc.) displayed in a visual row
- **Testimonials section** — Customer reviews in a clean card layout
- **FAQ section** — Accordion-style frequently asked questions
- **Footer** — Logo, contact info, social links, copyright

### 2. Product Page

- **Product image gallery** placeholder area (left side)
- **Product details** (right side) — Name, flavor, price, short description, quantity selector, "Order Now" button
- **Tabs below** — Nutrition Facts, Ingredients, How to Use, Reviews
- **Related info** — Benefits icons row

### 3.  Admin Page

- **Admin panel with access of product and it's purchase history and all**
- **Admin sidebar** — good admin design with sidebar add product, users who purchase products and change password etc

## Design Direction

- **Colors**: Teal/green primary (#0d9488), white background, dark text, lime green accents for highlights
- **Typography**: Bold headings, clean sans-serif body text
- **Style**: Modern, energetic, clean whitespace, rounded cards — inspired by Supply6
- **Logo**: My Fit Boat logo embedded from uploaded image
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly
